About ICHEC

ICHEC is organized by the Department of Chemical & Biochemical Engineering, IIT Patna. The conference focuses on the latest research in separation, purification, catalysis, process intensification, and biochemical processing.

ICHEC brings together academia and industry through keynotes, invited talks, workshops and poster sessions.
